Welcome Back!

Dear Faculty, Staff and Students,
The new semester is practically at our doors! Since most of our operations have been in person at the VRC, we wanted to take a moment to welcome everybody back, and welcome newcomers. Our new Curator of Collections, Rémi Castonguay joined our team on June 1st and has been working hard to learn about the wonderful AHVA team and resources.

Services

The VRC offers a variety of services

    Image research/acquisition/production
    Digital imaging on demand (e.g., from print publications)
    Digital exhibits support
    Digital humanities project support
    Grant writing support (e.g. for technical language pertaining to imaging and technology
    Descriptive metadata (e.g., best practices to manage your data)
    Instruction/tutorials (e.g., copyrights, finding images, research tools, etc.)
    Presentation assistance
    Desktop/laptop computer usage
    Seminar/screening room (by appointment)
    AV equipment/set up< and troubleshooting/ul>

    Please inquire with Rémi Castonguay to find out more or request our services

The VRC is Starting an Long-Term Assessment Process

Over the summer we started an extensive reorganisation of supplies, equipment and collection. Our emphasis is to create a welcoming space for students, faculty and staff, expecially considering the scarcity of meeting rooms at Lasserre. During Windert I & II we will hold a vast consultation on space and services needs among the AHVA community. In the meantime, stay tuned!
